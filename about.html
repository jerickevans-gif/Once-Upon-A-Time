Our Vision & Mission

OUAT envisions a community where children and families use dance, music, and storytelling to express themselves, connect with one another, and create shared experiences that bring people together.

We exist to provide children and families across Queens with accessible and creative arts education. Our programs build creativity, teamwork, and self-expression in a supportive learning environment that help children grow both on and off the stage.

Our Values

OUAT dedicates its support through core programs and community engagement.

Providing programs and seasonal courses in dance, theater, and art that inspire creativity.

Engaging with communities through performances, workshops, and cultural arts experiences.

Supporting children's growth with skilled instructors who foster story-telling, skill mastery, and personal growth.
