Our Vision & Mission

OUAT envisions a community where children feel welcome and free to dance, sing, and storytelling to express themselves, connect with one another, and create shared experiences that bring people together. We exist to provide children and families across Queens with accessible and creative arts education. Our programs build creativity, teamwork, and self-expression in a supportive learning environment that helps children grow both on and off the stage.

Our Values

OUAT dedicates its support through core programs and community engagement.

Creative Programs

Providing programs and seasonal courses in dance, theatre, and art that inspire creativity in young people.

Community Engagement

Engaging with communities through performances, workshops, and cultural arts experiences across Queens.

Personal Growth

Supporting children's growth with skilled instructors who foster discipline, mastery, and personal growth.
