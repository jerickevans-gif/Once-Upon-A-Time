Privacy Policy

Last updated April 29, 2026

Once Upon A Time, Inc. ("OUAT", "we", "us") is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving children and families in Queens, NY. This policy explains what information we collect from visitors and program participants, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account or enroll a child in a program, we collect name, email, phone, billing address, and child profile details (name, age, allergies, special conditions, emergency contact). Payment is processed by our PCI-compliant payment processor; we do not store full card numbers.

Usage information

We use first-party analytics to understand site usage. We do not sell or share personal information with third-party advertisers.

How we use it

  • To provide programs, classes, and donor services you've requested
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, class reminders, password resets)
  • To send program updates and our newsletter — you may unsubscribe at any time
  • To meet legal and tax-reporting obligations as a registered nonprofit

Your rights

You may request a copy of, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing contact@onceuponatime.org. California, EU, and similar jurisdictions have additional rights under applicable law; we honor those requests.

Cookies

We use a small number of essential cookies (session, language, accessibility) and an optional analytics cookie. You can opt out at any time via the cookie banner or by clearing your browser cookies.

Children

Programs are designed for children, but accounts are held by parents or legal guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 outside of the parent/guardian flow.

Contact

Questions? Reach our privacy lead at contact@onceuponatime.org or 87-61 111th St, Richmond Hill, NY 11418.
