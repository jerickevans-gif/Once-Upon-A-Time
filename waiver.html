Participant Agreement

Once Upon A Time, Inc. — Effective Apr 29, 2026

This Participant Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Once Upon A Time, Inc., a New York 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation ("OUAT"), and the parent or legal guardian ("you") of the named participant ("Participant").

1. Acknowledgment of risk

Participant will engage in arts education programs that include physical activities such as dance, theater, and gardening. You acknowledge that these activities carry inherent risks of injury, including but not limited to strains, sprains, falls, and physical contact with other participants. You voluntarily assume those risks.

2. Release of liability

You release OUAT, its instructors, staff, volunteers, directors, and assigns from any claim, demand, or cause of action arising from ordinary negligence in connection with Participant's participation, except where prohibited by law.

3. Medical authorization

You authorize OUAT staff to seek emergency medical care for Participant if needed and confirm that the emergency contact and medical history information you've provided is accurate and current.

4. Field-trip permission

Some classes (notably the Gardening track) may include short field trips within walking distance of the studio. You authorize Participant to take part in these supervised excursions.

5. Photo and video release

You may grant OUAT permission to photograph or video-record Participant during programs for the limited purpose of documenting and promoting OUAT's mission. This permission is optional and revocable in writing at contact@onceuponatime.org.

6. Governing law

This Agreement is binding upon you, your heirs, and assigns, and is governed by the laws of the State of New York.

A signed digital copy of this waiver is captured during enrollment and stored with your customer record. You can request a copy at any time.
