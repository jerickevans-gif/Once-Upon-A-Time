Accessibility Statement

We're committed to digital and physical access for everyone.

Our commitment

Once Upon A Time, Inc. strives to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA for all digital content. We design, develop, and test with accessibility in mind, and treat every accessibility report as a priority bug.

Digital features

  • Keyboard navigation across every interactive element
  • Visible focus indicators that meet 3:1 contrast minimum
  • Semantic HTML and ARIA used to label form fields, regions, and live updates
  • Color contrast for body text meets 4.5:1; large text meets 3:1
  • Reduced-motion preference respected for animations and scrolling
  • Alt text on every meaningful image

Physical access

Our studio at 87-61 111th St, Richmond Hill, NY 11418 has step-free entry from the street, an accessible restroom, and wheelchair-accessible studios on the ground floor. If you have specific access needs for a class, please email us in advance.

Reporting an issue

Found something that doesn't meet the bar? Please email contact@onceuponatime.org with the page URL and a description. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Ongoing work

We test with screen readers (VoiceOver, NVDA), audit color contrast each release, and conduct an external accessibility review annually. Most recent review: April 2026.
