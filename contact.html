Answer to common questions!

How do I book a class?
Browse the schedule on the Programs page, pick a class, and click Enroll Now. You'll be guided through registration and payment in a few short steps. Confirmation arrives by email within minutes.
How many classes can I take?
There's no cap — most students attend two to three classes per week. We can recommend a balanced schedule based on your goals.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the class start time receive a full credit. Late cancellations may forfeit the session.
Are the tour prices per person or per group?
Tour prices are per person, with discounts for groups of six or more. Reach out for custom group quotes.

Donation questions? We've got answers!

How do I donate?
Visit our Donation page and choose a one-time gift or a recurring monthly contribution. We accept card, ACH, and donor-advised funds.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. Once Upon A Time, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. You'll receive an itemised tax receipt by email.
What is the cancellation policy?
Recurring donations can be paused or cancelled any time from your account preferences, or by replying to your monthly receipt.
Where does my donation go?
Roughly 87¢ of every dollar funds programs — scholarships, instructor pay, and venue costs — with the remainder covering admin and outreach.
