FAQs

What should my child bring to camp?

Comfortable clothing, a refillable water bottle, and a packed snack. We provide all program materials.

Are scholarships available?

Yes — partial and full scholarships are awarded on a rolling basis. Email contact@onceuponatime.org for the application.

Can I drop in for a single session?

Our seasonal camps require a 2-month commitment so students can build through the full arc, but private sessions are available for short-term enrollment.
