Our Story

Who We Are

Once Upon A Time, Inc. is a nonprofit organization dedicated to giving young people language and tools through the mediums of dance, music, art, and theater to tell stories that better shape the community around them. Incorporated in 1980 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, our mission is to implement arts-oriented childcare programs and enrichment programs for an underserved community.

Following the tragic loss of its founders and subsequent closure, this initiative seeks to restore and expand the organization's arts programs, re-engage with the community, and ensure the long-term sustainability of its impactful mission.

Empowering children through dance and theatre programs

Year-round performances that establish teamwork and skill development

Community-driven arts education rooted in inclusivity and care
