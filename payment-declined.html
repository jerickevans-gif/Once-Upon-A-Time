We couldn't process that payment

No charge was made. Common reasons: insufficient funds, expired card, or a CVV mismatch. You can retry with the same or a different method.

Retry payment Contact support

What to try

  • Double-check the card number, expiry date, and CVV
  • Make sure the billing ZIP matches the card statement
  • Try a different payment method (PayPal or Apple Pay)
  • If you've recently moved, your bank may need to authorize the new address

Still stuck? Email contact@onceuponatime.org with the time of the attempt and the last 4 digits of the card.
